01The series

Three briefings. Three positions.

Each briefing takes one question a board keeps circling and answers it plainly — what to decide, in what order, and what it costs to get wrong.

Briefing 01

AI adoption is not implementation

Buying licences puts AI in the building. It doesn't put AI to work. This briefing separates adoption from deployment — why tools in staff hands don't become organisational capability, and where the returns actually start.

Briefing 02

Designing agent authority

Before an agent touches your tools, someone has to decide what it may do alone, what it prepares for sign-off, and when it escalates. This briefing sets out the questions to answer before access is granted — not after.

Briefing 03

Executive intelligence systems

Most leaders drown in reporting and still lack the one page that matters. This briefing covers scheduled decision briefings and operating signals — how they cut the noise without hiding accountability.
