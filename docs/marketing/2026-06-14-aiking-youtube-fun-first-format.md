# AIKING YouTube — fun-first format

Goal: make the AIKING YouTube page feel like a game/show Ash actually wants to do, while still building trust with Australian business owners.

## Core shift

Do not make the channel feel like homework, lessons, or generic business education.

Make it feel like:

> A business leak-hunting show where Ash finds obvious money leaks in Australian businesses and shows the AI Agent fix.

Tone: curious, cheeky, sharp, practical, Australian, anti-guru.

## Main show format: AIKING Leak Hunt

Every episode answers one question:

> Where is this business leaking leads, time, or revenue?

Simple structure:

1. Pick a business category: roofer, beauty clinic, dentist, gym, physio, accountant, real estate agency, etc.
2. Find the visible leak: missed call path, weak booking page, slow quote flow, ignored DMs, no follow-up, confusing offer, no reactivation.
3. Give it a leak score out of 10.
4. Show the AI Agent fix in plain English.
5. End with: `If you want me to find yours, comment AUDIT.`

## Fun recurring segments

### 1. Leak of the Day
30-60 sec Short. One leak, one punchline, one fix.

Examples:
- `This roofer is paying for leads then making people wait 2 days for a reply.`
- `This clinic has a booking button... but no follow-up when someone hesitates.`
- `This business doesn't need more ads. It needs to stop dropping the leads it already has.`

### 2. AI Agent Boss Battle
Ash picks one annoying business bottleneck and designs the agent that beats it.

Examples:
- Missed-call boss
- Quote-follow-up boss
- Dead-lead reactivation boss
- Admin inbox chaos boss
- Booking no-show boss

### 3. Roast then Rebuild
Light roast, then practical fix. Never cruel, never naming-and-shaming private info.

Format:
- `I'm not attacking the business. I'm attacking the leak.`
- `Here's what's costing them money.`
- `Here's the AI Agent I'd deploy first.`

### 4. Tradie Tech Support
Shorts for trades/service businesses.

Examples:
- `If you're a tradie and your phone rings while you're on a roof, this is probably leaking money.`
- `If your quote follow-up is "I'll get back to them later", you're donating revenue to your competitors.`

### 5. Clinic Chaos Files
For clinics/aesthetics/allied health.

Examples:
- `If someone asks about price on Instagram and no one follows up, that's not a DM. That's a revenue leak.`
- `A clinic doesn't need AI to replace staff. It needs AI to catch the admin work humans keep drowning in.`

### 6. AI Toy or AI System?
Fast comparison series.

Examples:
- ChatGPT prompt = toy
- Lead-capture + follow-up + CRM update + owner alert = system
- Random chatbot = toy
- Appointment recovery agent = system

## Creator game rules

Make it easy enough to do even when motivation is low.

### Minimum daily upload
One 30-second Short:

1. Say the business type.
2. Say the leak.
3. Say the AI Agent fix.
4. CTA: `comment AUDIT`.

### No overproduction rule
If it takes more than 20 minutes to record, it is too complicated for the early channel.

### Wheel of leaks
Pick one per video:

- Missed calls
- Slow reply
- Weak offer
- Bad booking page
- No quote follow-up
- No reactivation
- Ignored DMs
- Admin bottleneck
- Review/reputation leak
- No owner alerts

### Scoring system
Use repeatable numbers because it makes the videos feel like a game:

- Leak severity: /10
- Ease to fix: /10
- First agent to deploy
- Estimated complexity: Low / Medium / High

Avoid guaranteed ROI language. Keep it as a diagnostic score, not a financial promise.
