Governance artefacts

Operating-model documents leaders can actually use.

  • Authority matrix

    Defines what the system may draft, recommend, decide, trigger or escalate.

  • Risk register

    Captures model, data, vendor, workflow and reputational risks.

  • Evaluation plan

    Defines what good output looks like and how failures are reviewed.

  • Oversight cadence

    Sets review rhythm, ownership, reporting and improvement loops.
