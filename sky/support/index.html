Sky App — Support

Help when you need it.

For account, subscription or realtime voice problems, use the steps below or email support directly.

Email Sky support →

Sign in with Apple.

Make sure you are signed into your Apple Account on the iPhone and that the device has an internet connection. Sky never asks for your Apple password. If Apple sign-in is temporarily unavailable, wait briefly and try again.

Subscription and restore.

If you already subscribed but Sky still shows the subscription screen, tap Restore purchases. Confirm that the iPhone is using the same Apple Account that purchased Sky Monthly. Manage or cancel at Apple Account subscription settings.

Microphone and voice.

Open iOS Settings, find Sky, and make sure Microphone access is enabled. Check that the iPhone is online, then start a fresh conversation. Speaker mode changes the audio route; it does not change the selected AI voice.

For privacy, the public Sky service does not retain raw audio or conversation transcripts after the live provider session ends.

Delete your account.

Open the Account menu inside Sky and choose Delete account. This removes the Sky account data and invalidates existing Sky sessions. It does not cancel the Apple subscription, which must be managed separately through Apple.

What to include in a support email.

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • What you were trying to do and the exact on-screen error.
  • Whether Sign in with Apple, Restore purchases or a voice session was affected.

Never email passwords, Apple authorization codes, payment-card details, recovery codes or API keys.
