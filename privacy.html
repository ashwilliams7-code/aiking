Privacy & collection notice

Confidential by default.

AIKING treats executive enquiries and commercially sensitive context as confidential. This notice sets out, plainly, what this website collects, what it doesn't, and how enquiry information is handled.

What this website collects.

This is a static website. The briefing form prepares an email draft on your own device — nothing you type is transmitted to or stored by the site. No submission exists until you choose to send that email yourself.

Information you may choose to send.

  • Your name, role, company and email address.
  • Your timeframe and where AI currently sits in your business.
  • The commercial or operational outcome you want to discuss.

How AIKING uses enquiry information.

Information you send is used for three things only: to assess fit for a private briefing, to respond to your enquiry, and to prepare the scoping conversation that follows. It is read personally by Ash Williams. No mailing list, no resale, no third-party marketing.

Do not send passwords, credentials, or confidential documents through the enquiry form. Initial qualification never requires them. Secure channels for sensitive material are established during engagement scoping.

Confidentiality.

Executive enquiries are handled discreetly. Detailed data-handling, confidentiality and security terms are agreed in engagement documentation before any diagnostic or implementation work begins — so the obligations are in writing before your data moves.

Corrections and contact.

To update or remove information you have sent, email ash@aiking.info. Requests are actioned directly by the founder.

AIKING is a private AI advisory for Australian CEOs, directors and executive teams — strategy, deployment and governance under one advisor. Enquiries: ash@aiking.info.
